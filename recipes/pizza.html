Easy Tortilla Pizza

This tortilla pizza is extremely easy to make

It is light enough to be a snack, serves well as an appetizer, or is so good that it can be devoured alone!

Tortilla Pizza Ingredients

These are the ingredients you will need for this homemade tortilla pizza recipe:

  • 1 (8 inch) soft flour tortilla
  • 1 tea spoon olive Oil
  • 3 tablespoons tomato sauce
  • 1 pinch garlic powder
  • 1 cooked chicken breast, sliced
  • Half green bell pepper, chopped
  • 2 green onions, finely chopped
  • Half cup shredded mozzarella cheese
  • 1 pinch dried oregano

Steps on How to Make Pizza With a Tortilla

  1. Brush the tortilla with olive oil, season, and bake.
  2. Top with tomato sauce and toppings. Sprinkle it with cheese.
  3. Bake again until the cheese is melted, then season it with oregano.
